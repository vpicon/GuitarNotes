\chapter{Chords and Triads}
As done in Walter Piston's tome. The combination of two or more harmonic intervals makes up a \textit{chord}. The basic chord of common-practice harmony is the \textit{triad}, a group of three tones (called \textit{chord tones}) obtained by placing one third on top of another.
